About the webinar

The COVID-19 pandemic is driving the critical need for cutting-edge technological tools and innovation in the areas of public health, medicine, and wellness. The global big data in the healthcare market is forecast to total more than $34 billion by 2022 bringing the most notable and big changes in the industry. What was previously done in years and months can now be done in a matter of weeks.

Join the webinar to learn how leveraging Big Data can improve clinical outcomes, boost workforce productivity and improve the revenue stream for hospitals and healthcare organizations.

During a one-hour live session with experts you will:

  • Explore the major challenges the healthcare industry face today and the main trends that will affect the world of big data analytics in the market
  • Discover the top Big Data applications in healthcare and how to use data analytics to transform processes in personal care, clinical care, and public health
  • Deep-dive into practical cases of how healthcare organizations explored new business models and achieved more efficient decision-making, greater efficiency, and, as a result, process optimization
