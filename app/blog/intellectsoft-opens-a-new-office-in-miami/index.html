Intellectsoft Opens a New Office in Miami 

April 26, 2021
  • Home
  • Blog
  • Business
  • Intellectsoft Opens a...
Book a Consultation Now!Contact us

Intellectosft is proud to announce that we have opened our brand new office in Miami.

Right now, the city is a hub of all the latest blockchain solutions and since our company has a strong focus on this technology, from now on, we have a unique opportunity to stay on top of the latest innovations.

It enables Intellectsoft to keep growing our expertise and industry knowledge as well as offer high-end solutions for clients across various industries.

Our team is a digital transformation consultancy and software engineering company that is already 14 years in operations.

Intellectsoft provides cutting-edge solutions for global organizations and enterprises, with the mission to accelerate the adoption of new technologies, untangle complex issues that always emerge during digital evolution, and orchestrate ongoing innovation.

We collaborate with clients to improve operational efficiency, enhance customer satisfaction, drive more revenue, and take businesses to the next level through digitalization.

Our team has successfully delivered amazing solutions for Fortune500 companies, and such renowned giants as EY, Jaguar, Harley Davidson, Nestle, Eurostar, Lang Rover, Guinness, and others.

Intellectsoft’s professionalism and industry expertise has been recognized by Clutch, Inc. 5000, TheManifest, Digital.com, and ITfirms.

Need full-scale technological solutions? Don’t hesitate to contact us and tell us all about the project so we can reach back to you immediately with the best matching ideas.
